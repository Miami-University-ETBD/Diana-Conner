The Mouser's Annual Cheese Raffle!

Information

Ticket Info.
Every mouse on the factory floor gets two tickets for the raffle, while the delivery and order mice get one ticket each.

Since Mr.Mousers untimely death in 1351, Carlyle Mouse wanted to honor Mr. Mouser's Business every November with a cheese raffle for all of our loyal workers around the world and in the cheese factory making his dreams a reality.

Raffle Rules

  1. You are NOT allowed to barter tickets with a work buddy to have a bigger chance of winning the raffle, if you get caught doing such heinous activities, the Head Mouser will have no choice but to terminate you.

  2. Sharing prize cheese with coworkers is prohibited. Any acts of "kindness" will be considered conspiracy against the company and will be punished.

  3. If you participate, you are under contract not to sue us for any damage that may occur. If you win, we are in no way, shape or form liable for anything that happens to you.

Winner's Benefits

Benefits include a handshake from the CEO, a "congradulations" from the head mouser, and two days of vacation time for a year!
